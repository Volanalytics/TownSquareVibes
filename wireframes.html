About Wilson Cafe

Wilson Cafe is a locally-owned coffee shop in the heart of downtown Lebanon. We pride ourselves on serving premium coffee sourced from ethical suppliers, along with homemade pastries and breakfast items.

Established in 2018, we've become a community hub where locals gather to work, socialize, and enjoy quality beverages. Our team of baristas are trained to craft the perfect cup every time.

We're proud to support other local businesses by sourcing ingredients locally whenever possible and participating in community events.
